Meet the team

John Inhyon Park DC, L.Ac, MS

Dr. John Inhyon Park has over 25 years of experience in physical medicine and functional restoration through his ongoing passion for patient care and professional development. John earned his Doctorate in Chiropractic from the New York Chiropractic College. While incorporating traditional Chiropractic techniques of spinal adjustments, corrective exercise and postural alignment, he takes on a holistic approach through Eastern medicine and Nutrition. John received his Master of Science (MS) in Acupuncture from Tri-State College of Traditional Chinese Medicine and his MS in Nutrition from the University of Bridgeport. Dr. Park’s approach to wellness and healing is through compassion and understanding. His goal is to stimulate healing and prevent illness by promoting overall good health through the balance of physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual health, which ultimately leads to an optimum quality of life.

David Park PT, DPT

Dr. David Park earned his Bachelor’s degree in Psychology with a concentration in organizational psychology and finance from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. He worked in the financial services industry but despite the financial rewards, the one major thing that was lacking was a sense of fulfillment and internal reward. He ultimately left the corporate world to pursue his true passion in Physical Therapy and movement science, where he can make a difference in people's every day lives. David earned his Doctorate in Physical Therapy from Hunter College in New York City. Among the many schools of thought for patient care, David has adhered to the Functional Manual Therapy (FMT) approach through the Institute of Physical Art (IPA). David has taken extensive training through the IPA and is working towards the prestigious certification in FMT. David also obtains ongoing mentorship and is part of the team at IPA Manhattan Physical Therapy. As an avid golfer, he is certified through the Titleist Performance Institute (TPI) to evaluate and treat golf related injuries.
